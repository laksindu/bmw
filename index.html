About

BMW, short for Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, is a globally renowned German luxury automobile and motorcycle manufacturer, celebrated for its innovation, precision engineering, and performance-driven vehicles. Founded in 1916, BMW initially produced aircraft engines before transitioning to automobiles and motorcycles. Headquartered in Munich, Germany, BMW has become synonymous with elegance and cutting-edge technology, offering a range of premium cars, including the iconic BMW 3 Series, 5 Series, and the high-performance M Series. The company is also a pioneer in sustainable mobility, leading the way with its electric and hybrid vehicles under the BMW i sub-brand, such as the BMW i3 and i8. Known as "The Ultimate Driving Machine," BMW emphasizes driver-focused design, blending luxury with dynamic performance. Its rich motorsport heritage, highlighted by achievements in Formula 1 and endurance racing, further cements its status as a leader in the automotive world. Beyond vehicles, BMW is committed to sustainability, innovation, and creating a connected driving experience, solidifying its reputation as one of the most respected and aspirational automotive brands globally.
